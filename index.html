Welcome! Let's talk about Weddings...

I'm David Rohr - Dave to my friends. And I'm a florist - a third-generation florist, in fact. And one of the things I love to do is create beautiful weddings for special people - like you.

Janet & James

The Parker is a very special place, and Janet had a very special vision - the combination of desert succulents with gold and red.

Her stunning bouquet was a great contrast with her delicate lace gown, and the colored handle picked up the color of the lanterns at the ceremony site.

Chelsey & John

Romance on a hillside - The O’Donnell House was the setting for Chelsey and John’s wedding. Chelsey’s vision included pink peonies - which made for a stunning bouquet.

Her bridesmaids carries hot pink, making a strong statement, while letting the bride stand out from the smiling crowd.
